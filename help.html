Welcome to Draggable

A visual tool for mapping, planning, and arranging your ideas

What is Draggable?

Draggable is a drag-and-drop web-based tool that allows you to visually map out processes, brainstorm ideas, or arrange snippets of information. Its flexible interface and smooth usability make it perfect for both quick diagrams and in-depth visual thinking sessions.

Get Started Quickly

Not sure where to begin? Try using our custom ChatGPT model Draggable Templates. This assistant helps you create templates and layouts which can then be imported directly into Draggable.
